Private facts demo

Here you can try out the Tahoe-LAFS public test grid.

Note that use of tahoe in this way does not adhere to the core Tahoe concept of provider-independent security for a number of reasons:

1. You are transmitting your unencrypted data to us over the web;

2. the encryption and storage is happening on our server; and

3. the capability string for retrieving the data is known to and processed by our server.

This app is only for demonstration purposes, and you should not expect either security or persistence for the data uploaded to it.

To gain the actual security benefits of Tahoe, you should run your own server.
